Kinship Care

Data from the 2014 Census show that over 600,000 children in Texas live with a grandparent. With mounting pressure on lawmakers to solve the problems facing Texas’ foster care system, caregiving grandparents like Mercedes could be an important part a plan to keep kids with family. Unfortunately for Mercedes and other extended family (or "kinship") caregivers in Texas, those who step up to raise a family member’s children often face significant financial hardship and do not receive enough support.
