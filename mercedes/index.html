Mercedes

Mercedes never imagined raising five young children at age 62. But taking in her grandchildren when they were neglected was the only choice in her mind. Unfortunately for her and other extended family caregivers in Texas, those who step up to raise a family member’s children often face significant financial hardship and do not receive enough support.

Despite these challenges, Mercedes says, “I have to think, how can I best serve my children?”

Each morning, after getting all the kids to school, Mercedes goes to church. Then she meets with other grandparents taking care of their grandkids and advocates trying to help support them.

Mercedes used to work, but the obligations of taking care of her grandchildren made it impossible for her to keep her job.
